About

Chandigarh University is a full-fledged university established by the Punjab State Legislature and is recognized by University Grants Commission under Section 2(f) with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22(1) of the UGC Act, 1956.
The name of the university is inspired by The City Beautiful – Chandigarh, known as the epitome of cultural heritage and urbanization.
As a student-centric university, CU provides optimal learning experiences and services that lead to student success. We empower our students to take responsibility for their academic work, social life and career development.

Vision & Mission

Vision:To be globally recognized as a Centre of Excellence for Research, Innovation, Entrepreneurship and disseminating knowledge by providing inspirational learning to produce professional leaders for serving the society
Mission:Ensuring students delight by meeting their aspirations through blended learning, corporate mentoring, professional grooming, flexible curriculum and healthy atmosphere based on co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

Academics

Chandigarh University's academic focus isn't just about learning; it's about empowerment. From embracing diversity and emerging technologies to offering interdisciplinary insights and blending expertise with practicality.
Training and certification for in-demand skills not only enhance your degree but allow you to have a valued credential reflected on your resume. A Nano degree, provided by CU and partners allows you to earn certification, from basic skills to advanced technologies along with the regular degree.
Chandigarh University has tied up with FOSS, IIT Bombay which allows students undergo training in a range of upcoming technologies through online mode. With three courses in each semester this open source allows you to hone your skills in latest software such as PHP, MySQL, Java Business Application, BASH, Pearl, RUBY and many more. Students upon successful completion receive certification from CU and IIT Bombay
